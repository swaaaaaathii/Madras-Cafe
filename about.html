Madras Cafe Inc. is a South Indian restaurant located in the city of Chennai, in several locations.

The concept behind Madras Cafe, a restaurant serving quality South Indian food at affordable prices, was first born several years ago. South Indian food has always been popular for its flavours and variety. Thus, Madras cafe was established, to provide affordable and tasty South Indian food.
